Title: Determining how one concept is related to another

Overview

In this task, you will be helping scientists determine what kind of relationship exists between two biomedical concepts according to a sentence.

The Task

  1. Read a given sentence. Two concepts, the subject and the object have been highlighted in the sentence. The definitions of the concepts are provided for your reference.
  2. Determine what the sentence says about how the subject is related to the object.
  3. Rate how confident you are in your answer.

Examples

  1. [subject] is physically a part of [object]:
    • The mitochondria, which is found in the cytoplasm of a cell, is responsible for generating the cell's energy.
  2. [subject] is the location of [object]:
    • The ribosome is the cellular organelle where protein synthesis occurs.
  3. [subject] comes before [object] in time:
    • Patients suffering from Barbett's syndrome first received acupuncture prior to a follow up MRI.
  4. [subject] is a process of, positively or negatively affects, or brings about [object]:
    • Homeostasis, the set of all processes which maintain a constant internal environment for an organism, is evidently not unique to just humans.
    • Runners with fractures suffer a dramatic decrease in their running ability.
    • The protein p53 is one of the best implicated factors involved in cancer.
  5. [subject] is a type of, given to, or occurs together with [object]:
    • Crohn's disease, an autoimmune disorder where the immune system attacks the gastrointestinal tract, currently has no cure.
    • While flobantimab is frequently perscribed to diabetic patients, whether the drug actually has any effect is unknown.
    • For a long time, doctors have observed that patients with hair loss also have persistent coughs, but why this is the case is unknown.

Making your judgement

Please base your answers on what is stated in the provided sentence.
